To make the booking process easier, please follow the guidelines below:

Pricing

Send me a direct message with the following details:

  • Reference image or flash design
  • Body placement
  • Approximate size (in cm)

With this information, I can provide a price estimate.

Booking and Deposit

A 20% deposit is required to secure your appointment. This amount will be deducted from the total cost of your tattoo. The deposit is non-refundable. If you cancel or miss your session, a 5% rescheduling fee will be applied.

Waitlist

When I open my schedule for other regions, I start accepting bookings for those locations. Once dates are confirmed, I notify clients on the waitlist in advance, giving them priority in choosing their appointment time.

On the Day of Your Session

  • Eat well and stay hydrated
  • Avoid alcohol the day before
  • Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area
